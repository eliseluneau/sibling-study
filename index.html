Sibling Position Study

How do you actually behave?

🔒 100% anonymous & confidential

This test collects no name and no identifying information. Your answers are stored anonymously as part of a research project on birth order and behaviour. If you choose to share your email at the end, it is used only to send you your results — it is never linked publicly to your responses. You can stop at any time.

You'll move through 38 questions in two formats. Some place you in a real-life situation and ask how you'd respond — pick the one that's most honest, not the one that sounds best. Others present a short statement and ask how strongly you agree. There are no right or wrong answers. The patterns are invisible during the test.

Takes about 10–14 minutes.
